From 1945 onwards - and throughout the economic boom - the Ambrosian diocese was elevate with churches. In Milan itself, were built more then 300. In that historical moment, the spaces were an expression of the experimentation of famous architects. The project is an attempt to investigate the well-known works of architects among the cities of Milan, Varese and Pavia.

Incomplete Itinerary - 24 Contemporary Churches in Lombardy traces an itinerary that lead to an unordinary path. In both the exhibition and the publication, the viewer is guided by a geographical map that locates the ecclesiastical buildings in the three areas explored, describing the traced "incomplete itinerary".
